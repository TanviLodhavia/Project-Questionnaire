8. Write the steps to test your first designed app in the online editor on mobile.

  1. Download the app expo.io on your android/ios phone
  2. Login to the same account you are using on the web
  3. Once you are logged in go to the app you want to try out on your phone
  4. Press the button android/ios based on your mobile phone. That button is found above the temporary display
  5. Once you press there, it will give you two options, tap to play and run on your device
  6. Choose run on your device
  7. It will give you 3 options: Device ID, Account, QR Code
  8. Choose the QR code option
  9. Now go to the app expo on your mobile phone
  10. In projects, first you will find an option of Scan QR Code
  11. Click on it
  12. Scan the QR Code shown on your PC Screen
  13. And it will take you to the app you have created on your phone
